# ✅ **1. WEATHER & CLIMATE SCENARIOS**

### **1.1 Sudden Weather Changes**

Scenarios:

* Temperature spikes
* Temperature drops
* Sudden humidity drop
* Sudden humidity rise
* Sudden wind gusts
* Cloud cover appearing/disappearing
* Rapid solar radiation changes

Feed to model:

* `temp(t-1..n), humidity(t-1..n), wind, solar, ET0, VPD trend`

Model reaction:

* Recalculate ET rate
* Re-estimate moisture loss curve

* Adjust irrigation automatically (increase/decrease cycle)

# ✅ **2. SOIL BEHAVIOR SCENARIOS**

### **2.1 Different Soil Types**

Plant type doesn’t matter; soil behavior does.

Scenarios:

* Sandy soil (drains fast)
* Clay soil (holds water, slow to dry)
* Loam (balanced)
* Rocky soil
* Organic-rich soil

Feed to model:

* Soil infiltration rate
* Water retention curve
* Soil moisture response after irrigation vs after rain

Model reaction:

* Dynamic prediction of moisture decay
* Auto-calibration of soil hydrology model

---

### **2.2 Soil Saturation & Dryness Events**

Scenarios:

* Overly dry (below wilting point)
* Below optimal moisture
* Near field capacity
* Fully saturated
* Waterlogging

Feed to model:

* Moisture thresholds
* Drainage coefficient
* Past irrigation timing

Model reaction:

* Prevent overwatering
* Skip irrigation if waterlogged
* Plan recovery irrigation if soil becomes too dry

---

### **2.3 Sensor Drift / Sensor Error**

Scenarios:

* Moisture sensor stuck at low
* Moisture sensor stuck at high
* Noisy sensor output
* Delayed sensor reading

Feed to model:

* Sensor calibration profiles
* Expected value ranges
* Redundant sensor validation

Model reaction:

* Smooth data with filters (Kalman/EMA)
* Fall back on prediction model
* Detect abnormal values
